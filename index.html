Biography + Quotes

Electro Folk-Pop. The Tame sing like Wolves.

Familiar Wild writes "pop music with heart and soul...and brain...and kindness" (DJ Champion). The newest album (nominated for a Toronto Indie Music Award and Western Canadian Music Award) could be the love child of Indie Folk band Daughter, Electro-based Postal Service and Scandi-Pop artist Susanne Sundfor. Familiar Wild performs alongside meticulously curated video, putting audiences "under a dreamlike spell" of lingering joy (Vancouver Weekly) and creating "a tribe-like atmosphere" with "deep electro-synth influences" (Impose Magazine).
